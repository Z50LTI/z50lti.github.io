About

OUR STORY

As a food scientist, Nicholas Jose’s usual routine involves compounding citrus oils, handling liquid nitrogen, and participating in triangle tests. Although he was adept at what he does from 9-5, at the end of the day, he left work with a missing feeling he couldn’t quite put his finger on.

Then, in mid-2022, Nicholas was struck with a compelling idea:

“What if I started a mobile bar business?”

This was the question that ultimately got the snowball rolling. The thought of starting your own business and becoming your own boss was an intimidating thought, to say the least, but Nicholas knew that this opportunity was exactly what he was looking for.

Blending citrus oils for a corporation was one thing, but mixing, shaking, stirring, and even torching cocktails for enthusiastic guests at a party was an experience no lab could ever copy.

And so, in July 2022, Nicholas founded The Silver Glass Mobile Bar, LLC; and from September 2022 to February 2023, he – with some help from his younger brother, who is skilled in woodcrafting – built the “Silver Bar”, a full-service bar with wheels and a self-contained sink system.

We thank you so much for having an interest in us. No matter what the occassion is, The Silver Glass Mobile Bar is always looking forward to serve you and your guests, as well as to share unforgettable memories and experiences!

GETTING STARTED

STEP 1: PLANNING

To get started, first check to see if 1) your venue’s policy allows outside vendors, 2) there is enough space for our mobile bar to setup, and 3) the mobile bar has access to power, or if an electric generator will be needed. Once you have gathered all the details for your event, fill out and submit the form found here.

STEP 2: BOOKING

Upon receiving your form, we will check our availability and respond to your inquiry within 48 hours by emailing you a contract and a detailed quote. Once we receive both the signed contract and the 50% non-refundable retainer fee, your event will be booked.

STEP 3: CONSULTATION

Two weeks before the date of your event, we will email you a shopping list of what recommended quantities and brands of alcohol you should purchase for your finalized cocktail menu. The amount and brand of alcohol are only suggestions and can be purchased from any liquor store of your choice.

STEP 4: FINAL PAYMENT

Two weeks before the date of your event, we will email you an invoice for the final amount due. Final payment information can be found here.

STEP 5: EVENT DAY

Two hours (or one day) before your event, we will arrive at the venue to retrieve the alcohol and prepare the bar area. (If the client chose to pay the final amount of the invoice in cash, we will collect the final payment before setting up the bar.) Once everything has been set up, the bar is officially open!

STEP 6: CONCLUSION

One hour (or one day) after the conclusion of your event, we will begin breaking down and cleaning up the bar area. The alcohol you purchased will be set aside for you in a designated area. If you have saved your receipt, any unopened bottles of alcohol can be returned for a refund – or saved for the next party!
