What is Atmos Spatial Audio?

Dolby Atmos Spatial Audio is a new surround-sound format that allows for a more realistic and immersive audio experience. It's no secret that all major recording artists release their songs using this cutting-edge technology, and now you can too. With support from over 1000 playlists, fans can enjoy music the way it was meant to be heard. Trust our skilled engineers for the ultimate music mixing experience.

How does it work:

  • Using the final, high quality wav or mp3 version of your song & its mixed stems, we mix the various audio elements into a 3D space that emulates the experience of the music being played live in a room right in front of you.

  • Usually, if an artist does not have access to their mixed stems, they would not be able to have their song mixed in Atmos. With SPATIO, you can send your instrumental and acapella and we will emulate the stems using our proven mixing technique.

  • Once you verify and upload your files they will be sent directly to one of the experienced engineers in our Atmos Certified Studios. The result will be a masterfully done Atmos mix to deliver to your distributor.
