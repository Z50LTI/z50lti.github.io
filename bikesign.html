BIKESIGN TAKEOVER

Finally, you’ll find bikes on bike lanes.

BBDO Germany and fahrrad.de turned analogue traffic signs into digital touchpoints. Düsseldorf, March 5th, 2020 - fahrrad.de is one of the largest multichannel online bicycle dealers in Germany. So, in order to continue being where bikers are, BBDO Düsseldorf and fahrrad.de did something very special: Together we developed the BIKE-SIGN TAKEOVER project. From now on, Germany’s 100,000 simple traffic signs will become exclusive out-of-home touchpoints for fahrrad.de. Simply download the Snoopstar app from the app store, scan the bike label and discover exclusive, weekly changing, personalized offers from fahrrad.de. all exclusively available through these traffic signs.

For this more than 100,000 bike traffic signs across Germany were used.
